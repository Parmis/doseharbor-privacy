Frequently asked questions

My reminders aren’t appearing

Make sure notifications are enabled for DoseHarbor: open the iOS Settings app → DoseHarbor → Notifications and confirm Allow Notifications is on. Also check that Focus modes and Do Not Disturb aren’t silencing them.

How do I hide medication names on my lock screen?

Enable Discreet notifications in the app’s Settings. Reminders will then read “Time for your reminder” instead of showing medication names.

How do I back up or move my data?

Go to Settings → Export data (JSON) to get a complete copy of everything the app stores. Your data lives only on your device, so keeping an export is a good idea before switching phones.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings → Delete all data to permanently remove everything. Because data lives only on your device, deleting the app also deletes the data.

Is my data private?

Yes. DoseHarbor collects nothing and makes no network requests — everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Privacy

Not medical advice

DoseHarbor is a reminder tool, not medical advice. It does not diagnose, treat, or make recommendations about medications. Always follow your doctor’s or pharmacist’s guidance.
